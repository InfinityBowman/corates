Pricing models


Annual costing structure
⭐ Free Tier — Single-Study Appraisal (Always Free)
$0 forever
Perfect for students, faculty teaching appraisal, or anyone conducting a one-off study quality assessment.
Includes:
Appraise one study at a time
Complete any checklist
Automatic scoring for the study
PDF viewing + annotation
Save and export the single study’s scoring
No collaboration
No full project workspace
No visualizations or IRR
This tier builds familiarity with the tool and supports teaching and individual assessments.

⭐ Project Tier — Full Review Workspace
$49 per project (introductory pricing)
One project = one review with all features unlocked
Ideal for researchers conducting systematic or scoping reviews, graduate students working on theses/dissertations, or practice teams evaluating bodies of evidence.
Includes:
Full project workspace
Unlimited studies per project
Unlimited checklist use
Automatic scoring engine
Full visualizations (study-level + across-study)
Inter-rater reliability (IRR) calculations
Consensus workflows
Audit trails + decision documentation
PDF library + annotations
Export (CSV, PDF, structured JSON)
3 collaborators included for free
Additional collaborators: +$15/user
(Or upgrade to Team Tier)

⭐ Team Tier — For Labs, Centers, & Multi-Project Teams
$199 per year (per project owner)
Perfect for labs or research groups running multiple reviews throughout the year.
Includes everything in Project Tier, plus:
Unlimited projects
Up to 5 collaborators per project included
Admin tools (coming soon)
Priority support
Early access to new tools (ROB tools, additional checklists)
Additional collaborators: +$10/user

⭐ Institutional Tier — Universities, Agencies, Large Review Teams
Custom pricing ($1,500–$8,000/year depending on size)
Designed for universities, research centers, and organizations supporting multiple teams and large methodological workflows.
Includes:
Unlimited users
Unlimited projects
Centralized administration dashboard
Usage analytics (helpful for course evaluation or grant reporting)
Single sign-on (SSO) options
Department or university-wide license
Onboarding + training support
Integration pathways (API access roadmap)

CoRATES Monthly Pricing
A flexible monthly subscription model designed for researchers, students, and review teams.
Start free. Upgrade anytime.

⭐ Free Tier — Single-Study Appraisal (Always Free)
$0 / month
Perfect for learners, faculty teaching appraisal, or anyone needing a quick, one-off study assessment.
Includes:
Appraise one study at a time
Automatic scoring
Any checklist available in the platform
PDF viewing + annotation
Save/export the single-study scoring
Does not include:
Collaboration, projects, visualizations, IRR, audit trails

⭐ Project Plan — Full Review Workspace
$20 / month (cancel anytime)
Ideal for researchers conducting systematic, scoping, or rapid reviews, or any multi-study appraisal project.
Includes:
Unlimited projects
Unlimited studies per project
Automatic scoring engine
Full visualizations (study-level + across-study)
IRR calculations
Consensus workflows
Audit trails + decision tracking
PDF library + annotations
Up to 3 collaborators included
Additional collaborators: +$4/month per user

⭐ Team Plan — Labs, Centers, Research Groups
$39 / month (per project owner)
Built for labs or teams running multiple reviews throughout the year.
Includes everything in the Project Plan, plus:
Unlimited collaborators
Priority support
Early access to beta tools (new ROB tools, new checklists)
Admin tools (project oversight, user management)
This is the best option for active research teams with multiple ongoing or annual reviews.
